# APG Design and Privacy Principles (GDPR & APP)

This document maps the architectural and design decisions of the Aura Privacy Gateway (APG) to key privacy principles, specifically the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This demonstrates a "Privacy by Design" approach, where data protection is a foundational component of the system.

| **Art. 32**           | **Security of Processing**                    | APG employs a suite of modern, robust security controls to protect data: <br> - **Encryption in Transit:** Mandatory TLS 1.3 for all connections. <br> - **Encryption at Rest:** Ascon-128 AEAD for Zone B data. <br> - **Integrity:** AEAD authentication tags and Ed25519 signatures prevent tampering. <br> - **Key Management:** A secure KEK/DEK hierarchy with KMS/HSM integration protects the keys that protect the data. <br> - **Cryptographic Erasure:** Provides a secure method for data deletion. |
